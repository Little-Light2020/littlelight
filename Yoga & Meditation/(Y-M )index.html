Yoga & Meditation

Yoga is known to improve strength and flexibility, but it also has immense benefits for mental health ranging from improving mood, sharpening concentration, relieving stress and anxiety, and improving symptoms of depression and insomnia.


Yoga incorporates more aspects such as physical poses and breathing exercises than just meditation. Yoga also focuses on developing discipline, concentration and balance, just like meditation. While doing yoga, you will focus on physical exercises as well. Yoga is much more physical than just meditation. Here are the best yoga asanas for mental health.


Use these yoga poses for anxiety to calm down and find your center anytime:


Padmasana


Padmasana yoga is an ultimate meditative pose. It is a cross leg sitting asana, which follows the ancient Indian tradition of yoga with your feet on the thighs. This helps your digestion, spinal strength and improves calmness of the mind and also your mood.It helps keep your anxiety and depression at bay.

This is a meditative pose and is best when you do it with the early morning shine. The best time to refresh your mind and body.

The Triangle Pose
(Trikonasana Yoga)


This trikonasana exercise stretches and strengthens the muscles along with improving the functions of our body. This is a good yoga exercise for pregnant women. It helps in reducing blood pressure, stress and anxiety and also improves the functions of the blood through the entire body. This simple yoga asana improves our balance & concentration power. This asana also removes fats from the waist and thighs.

Reverse Warrior Pose


This is one of the yoga exercises names which does come in any list you check. We now already know about the Virabhadrasana or simply the warrior pose. This is just the reverse of it. Here we turn right on the reverse side of our bodies giving it a slight twist. This one is very useful in toning waist and abdomen muscles and also cures spine pains and other issues such as these.

Chakrasana


Chakrasana is very beneficial and effective if performed correctly. It keeps you active, physically fit and full of energy.

It stretches lungs, abdomen and thorax. Chakrasana engages the legs, shoulders, arms and pelvis. It is an energy booster yoga aasan. Chakrasana also called wheel pose and Urdhva Dhanurasana is a yoga pose that gives flexibility to the spine, stimulates energy flow in the entire body. It is called wheel pose as it forms wheel-like, semi-circle posture.
